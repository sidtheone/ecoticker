EcoTicker Design Directions

EcoTicker Design Direction Showcase

Eight distinct visual directions for the EcoTicker dashboard. Each uses the same locked palette, typography, and severity system, but explores a fundamentally different information hierarchy and visual personality.

Locked foundations: warm cream/dark slate palette, severity colors (BREAKING red, CRITICAL orange, MODERATE amber, INFORMATIONAL green), 3px left-border card accents, system-ui + monospace fonts, 1152px max-width.

Sample data: Amazon Deforestation (87, BREAKING, +12), Ocean Acidification (72, CRITICAL, +5), Air Quality Delhi (65, CRITICAL, -3), Arctic Ice Loss (45, MODERATE, +8), Coral Reef Decline (58, MODERATE, +2), Water Quality Index (24, INFORMATIONAL, -1).

Use the tabs above to explore each direction. Toggle dark mode with the moon icon (top right).

Direction 1: Bloomberg Terminal

Maximum data density. Pure information. Every pixel earns its place.

Key Characteristics

  • Table-based layout, no hero section
  • Ultra-thin 2px gauge bars
  • Dense ticker strip, compact rows
  • All topics visible without scrolling
  • Professional trading-floor aesthetic

Direction 2: Weather Radar Command Center

Dramatic, urgent, emergency-broadcast energy. The hero dominates the viewport.

Key Characteristics

  • Hero takes 40%+ of viewport with massive score
  • Thick 14px gradient gauge with marker
  • Red emergency-style ticker bar
  • Score numbers are 60-72px
  • Weather-emergency-broadcast feel

Direction 4: Minimal Nordic

Extreme restraint. Lots of white space. Calm, contemplative, Scandinavian design.

Key Characteristics

  • Hairline 1px gauge with tiny dot marker
  • Almost borderless cards, floating elements
  • Wide spacing, one idea at a time
  • Light typographic weight (300-400)
  • Meditative, unhurried information consumption

Direction 6: Card-Forward / Material

Elevated cards with shadows as the primary visual structure. Modern app dashboard, approachable and organized.

Key Characteristics

  • Cards with prominent box-shadows and elevation
  • Hero is just a larger, more prominent card
  • Medium gauge bar inside each card
  • Rounded corners, subtle depth layers
  • Self-contained severity units per card

Direction 7: Ticker-Dominant

The ticker IS the product identity. Stock trading platform energy. The crawl is king.

  • Massive full-width ticker band (not a thin strip)
  • Split layout: hero left (60%), movers right (40%)
  • Compact inline gauge next to score
  • 2-column card grid below
  • Stock trading platform identity
